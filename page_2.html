Your Guide to Timaru and South Canterbury Hi, I’m Morgan Nixon. Welcome to my digital guide to Timaru! I created this platform to showcase the absolute best of our beautiful South Canterbury region. From the stunning sands of Caroline Bay to our rich cultural heritage, I am here to help you uncover the local secrets, wild landscapes, and unique experiences that make our home so special. Why Explore Timaru With Me? True Local Insight: I share authentic, boots-on-the-ground knowledge of Timaru's best spots. Wildlife & Nature Guides: Learn where and when to spot our famous Little Blue Penguins safely. Curated Hidden Gems: Discover local walking tracks, historic Māori rock art, and top food spots. Community First: I proudly support and highlight our fantastic local businesses and heritage sites. My Travel Philosophy. I believe the best travel experiences happen when you get off the main highway. Timaru is a place rich in history, spectacular coastal views, and warm community spirit. Whether you are a Kiwi on a weekend road trip or an international traveler exploring the South Island, my goal is to help you fall in love with our region.Let's start exploring!
